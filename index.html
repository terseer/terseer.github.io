Brief Autobiography:


Hon. Tarkighir Dominic Dickson was born 47 years ago. He is currently a member representing Makurdi/Guma Federal Constituency in the Federal House of Representatives and a member of the All Progressives Congress (APC). He is an expert on Integrated Marketing & Business Management. His legislative interest includes security, education and public accountability. He is married with children.
